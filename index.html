    Welcome to WPI Esports!

    WPI Esports is the center for all things gaming at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. From on-campus tournaments to watch parties and a strong presence in the competitive collegiate scene, we have a ton of events to be a part of. We are proud to be competing in Overwatch, League of Legends, Rocket League, PUBG, Hearthstone, Heroes of the Storm, Rainbow 6: Siege, Counter Strike, Valorant, and anything else our community has a passion for.
